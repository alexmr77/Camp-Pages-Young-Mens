Labeling

    All clothing and equipment must be labeled with either sewin in labels, commercial heat pressed labels applied by the camp outfitter or permanent marker. Stick on labels are prohibited and must not be used as they detach from clothing in our dryers - making it impossible to return lost clothing items.
