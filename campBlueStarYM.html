Important Packing Notes

1- ALL CLOTHING AND EQUIPMENT MUST BE LABELED WITH CAMPER’S NAME.
2- WE STRONGLY RECOMMEND FOR CAMPERS ON BLUE STAR’S GROUP TRAVEL (SOUTH FLORIDA GROUP FLIGHT OR COACHES TO CHARLOTTE/ATLANTA) TO PURCHASE 1 LOGO SWEATSHIRT OR LONSLEEVE FOR TRAVEL DAYS. AIRPORTS AND COACHES CAN BE CHILLY AND CAMPER SAFETY IS OUR PRIORITY.
3- PLEASE ADHERE TO OUR TECHNOLOGY POLICY AND SHOE GUIDELINES HIGHLIGHTED IN THE FULL PARENT HANDBOOK. NO TECHNOLOGY (OTHER THAN MP3 PLAYERS) IS ALLOWED AT CAMP. ADDITIONALLY, FLIP-FLOPS, SLIDE- IN SANDALS WITHOUT ANKLE STRAPS, HIGH-HEELED SHOES, OR PLATFORM SHOES CAN COMPROMISE SAFETY AND INCREASE FALLS, SPRAINS, AND FRACTURES SO ARE PROHIBITED AT CAMP.
