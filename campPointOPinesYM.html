Special Activity Clothing

    Note: We have a number of special evening activities at camp. For some of these activities, campers are allowed to dress out of uniform. Although, we recommend limiting the amount of extra clothing your daughter brings to camp and we encourage the campers to be creative with their outfits for these special events, campers may want to consider bringing a few accessories or simple, inexpensive clothing for the following events:
      ☐ 50’s Night
      ☐ Western Night
      ☐ Aunt Emma’s Auction
      ☐ Viva la Fiesta Night
      ☐ Colorful Tops (any style, no need to buy something new, just bring from home) for Special Game Nights (assigned to wear Green, Orange or Pink)


Social Attire

    All clothing worn by Campers must be conservative and appropriate for our Camp setting. Not permitted: Tube tops, spaghetti strap tops, midriff exposing tops, fish net clothing, skirts or short shorts. For groups that have socials, attire is listed below.
      ☐ Inter A: Point O’Pines uniform
      ☐ Inter B: Point O’Pines uniform shorts, blue jeans or sweatpants with camper’s own casual non-uniform top.
      ☐ Sub: Same as Inter B social attire. Additionally, Sub Campers may wear white pants or shorts. No jean shorts are allowed.
      ☐ Senior and Peaker: Camper’s own casual, appropriate non-uniform attire.
