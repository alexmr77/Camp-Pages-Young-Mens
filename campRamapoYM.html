Clothing

    Dress at camp is casual and comfortable—please bring clothes you can move around in and that you don’t mind getting dirty
      ☐ Pair of Sneakers or Hiking Boots
      ☐ Jeans or Comfortable Pants
      ☐ Long and Short Sleeve Shirt
      ☐ Change of Underwear
      ☐ 2 Changes of Socks
      ☐ Pajamas
      ☐ Raincoat
      ☐ Sweater or Lightweight Jacket
      ☐ Hat or Hood
      ☐ Bath Towel
      ☐ Personal Toiletries for one night
      ☐ Water Bottle
      ☐ Flashlight
    We also recommend that you bring extra clothes, as nights at camp can get cool and often an extra change of clothes is helpful after an active, outdoor day


The Following Items are Not Allowed:

      ☐ Matches or Lighters
      ☐ Fireworks
      ☐ Knives, Weapons
      ☐ Pets


IMPORTANT: There is little to no cell phone reception on Ramapo’s campus. Inform participants, families, etc. that they most likely will NOT be able to give or receive calls while on camp. Participants who wish to set up a call in the evening through one of Ramapo’s landlines should bring phone cards.
