Clothing & Essentials

      ☐ 1 Pair of Casual Shoes
      ☐ 3 Pairs of Jeans and/or Sweatpants
      ☐ 5 Pairs of Shorts
      ☐ 10 Shirts (at least 7 T-shirts)
      ☐ 10 Pairs of Underwear
      ☐ 10 Pairs of Socks
      ☐ 1 Sweatshirt
      ☐ 1 Fleece/Jacket
      ☐ 1 Pair of Sneakers
      ☐ 1 Pair of Flip-Flops
      ☐ 2 Bathing Suits
      ☐ 2 Thin Shower Towels (for campground use)
      ☐ 1 Poncho (optional)
      ☐ 1 Laundry Bag (drawstring)


Additional Items

      ☐ Sleeping Bag in a “stuff sack”. We recommend a sleeping bag that is rated at approximately 30 degrees. (Don’t worry – it will never be that cold while we are camping this summer!)
      ☐ Camping Pillow (see “Gear-to-Go” flyer)
      ☐ Toiletries (soap, toothpaste, shampoo, etc.)
      ☐ Camera and Charger
      ☐ Cell Phone and charger
      ☐ Water Bottle
      ☐ Sunglasses
      ☐ Notebook to keep a Journal of your trip
      ☐ Flashlight
      ☐ Sunscreen


Luggage

      ☐ Duffel bag, not exceeding 30”L x 15”D x 14”H. A rolling duffel with handle and wheels is recommended.
      ☐ 1 “Carry-on” Bag, no larger than 21” x 10” x 12.” This bag will be carried on to the airplane, and will also ride with you in the bus.
      ☐ 1 Small Backpack (optional)
