Shabbos Dress Code

Boys: Button down or polo shirts, slacks, chinos or khakis. No blue jeans or sweatpants allowed. Casual shoes are fine. Sports sneakers, flip flops and baseball hats are not allowed for Shabbos.
Girls: Please make sure girls are sent with appropriate dresses for Shabbos. Sleeveless dresses or shirts may not be worn to shul for davening.
