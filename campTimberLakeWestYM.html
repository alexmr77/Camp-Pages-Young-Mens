3- FOR VISITING DAY, MANY PARENTS BRING A SUPER SOAKER OR WATER GUN TO CAMP FOR THE END OF VISITING DAY WATER FIGHT.
4- SOFT TRUNKS OR DUFFLES ONLY.
5 - DO NOT PACK MEDICATIONS- ALL MEDICATIONS MUST GO THROUGH MOUNTIAN MEDS.
