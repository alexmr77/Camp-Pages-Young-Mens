Important Notes

      Laundry is done weekly at our on-site facility and returned 1-2 days later.
      We cannot be responsible for damage to articles of clothing or missing articles. We encourage you to keep the more expensive things at home.
      Label everything, including shoes, baseball gloves, tennis racquets, hats and luggage.
      For younger campers, please send pre-addressed and stamped envelopes or postcards.
      Please use a laundry marking pen, name labels or clothing stamp to mark all items
      Campers may bring $15.00-$20.00 spending money in an envelope with your child's name on it, which will be kept in camp safe and can be used for necessities.
      Please pack all clothing in duffle bags or suitcases.
      Tranquillity Camp colors are Blue and White. These are the colors that are worn during camp Olympics.
