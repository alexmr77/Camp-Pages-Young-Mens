Important Packing Notes
1. All Clothing & Equipment must be labeled with camper's name.
2. Do not bring expensive valuables to camp! We will not be held responsible for lost or damaged items.
3. No bath of swim towels please - We cannot store or launder them.
4. Soft trunks or duffles ONLY.
5. Please do not bring any clothing that requires special care washing.
6. Do Not Overpack! Please follow the above guidelines, excessive items will not be unpacked and will be stored.
