IMPORTANT PACKING NOTES
1- ALL CLOTHING AND EQUIPMENT MUST BE LABLED WITH CAMPER’S NAME.
2- PLEASE DO NOT BRING EXPENSIVE VAULABLES TO CAMP! CAMP WESTMONT WILL NOT BE RESPONSIBLE FOR LOST OR DAMAGED ITEMS.
3- SOFT TRUNKS OR DUFFLES ONLY. HARD TRUNKS CANNOT BE ACCOMMODATED.
4- PLEASE DO NOT BRING CLOTHING THAT REQUIRES SPECIAL CARE WASHING.
5- BATTERY OPERATED FANS ONLY
6- DO NOT OVER PACK! PLEASE FOLLOW THE ABOVE GUIDELINES, EXCESSIVE ITEMS WILL NOT BE UNPACKED AND WILL BE STORED.
